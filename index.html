Why I like this Animal

I love raccoons because they are intelligent, clever problem solver, highly adaptable to different enviroments, and full of quirky personality that makes them fun to watch.

Cool Facts you don't know about this Animal

  1. Masters of Dexterity - Raccoons have incredibly nimble front paws with five fingers, almost like little hands.
  2. "Washing" their food - They're often seen dipping food in water before eating. Scientists think this isn't really washing but that wetting their paws help them better feel and identify object.
  3. City survivors - Raccoons thrive in urbans areas, sometimes more successfully than in the wild.
